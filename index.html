Dear Friends,

In this issue, we want to give you a glimpse of the many Mentors who build up our children and youth. At Udayan Care, mentoring forms a pillar of many of our programs, from the love and care given by our Mentor Parents to our Ghar children, to the spirit of giving back encapsulated by our Shalinis returning as Mentor Didis for their sisters. Mentors are the caregivers who nurture our children, the men and women who bring their wisdom to our Shalinis, the volunteers who give of their time and energy to ensure our kids’ success, even our own children and youth who take it upon themselves to guide their peers. Our mentors are legion and they are the foundation of the success of our Udayans.

So we invite you to hear their words and read their stories. And we hope you are as inspired by their stories as we are.

-Team Udayan Care
